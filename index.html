Trailblazer league tasks

Main features:

  • Two tabs to help you sort through thousands of tasks and stay sane.
  • Filterable list of all league tasks in one tab
  • Personal task list to pin the tasks you are working on in the second tab
  • As many sorting and organising features as possible

Runelite features:

  • Automatic completion of tasks, removing them from both lists as you do them
  • Option to automatically bring tasks of your current region to the top of your list
  • Option to automatically bring tasks you have recently got the level for to the top of your list

I won't be adding anything which takes away the fun or challange, so no QuestHelper style guides or optimal completion logic. Just organisation, helping you keep track of what you are working on and towards.



Everything is for mockup purposes only, sizing restrictions are based upon the space runelite offers.
